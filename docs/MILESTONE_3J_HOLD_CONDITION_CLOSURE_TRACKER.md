## Closure Rules

- A hold condition can close only after required evidence is provided and validated.
- Any `Rejected`, `Expired`, or `Not Provided` evidence keeps the condition open.
- Closure does not approve real-data execution.
- Closure does not schedule a load window.
- Closure does not constitute a Go/No-Go meeting outcome.
