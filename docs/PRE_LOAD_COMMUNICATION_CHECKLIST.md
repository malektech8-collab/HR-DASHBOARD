# Pre-Load Communication Checklist

This checklist registers the notifications required before executing any pilot load.

---

## 1. Pre-Load Notifications

*   [ ] **Authorization Ready Notice**: Verify that the final authorization package is signed off and logged in the register.
*   [ ] **Scope Confirmation Alert**: Notify the technical and business owners of the exact fields being uploaded.
*   [ ] **Scheduling Proposal Notice**: Send proposed load window times to the IT Ops team.
*   [ ] **On-Call Availability Call**: Confirm that the execution and rollback owners are available for the target time slot.
*   [ ] **Dashboard Freeze Warning**: Send email notifications to users alerting them of planned maintenance.
*   [ ] **Pre-Load Briefing**: Hold Go/No-Go operational alignment meeting 1 hour before window opens.
